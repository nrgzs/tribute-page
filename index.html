Hayao Miyazaki

Japanese animator, director, producer, screenwriter, author, and manga artist and a co-founder of Studio Ghibli.

Anime director Hayao Miyazaki speaks to reporters in March at his studio in Koganei, western Tokyo. Next to him is a poster for his film "Kaze Tachinu" ("The Wind Rises"), which was nominated for best animated feature at last year's Academy Awards.

Here's some facts about Hayao Miyazaki:

  • 1941- Born in January 5 Tokyo, Japan.
  • 1985- Hayao Miyazaki co-founded Studio Ghibli.
  • 2015- He wins Acedemy Awards.
  • Hayao Miyazaki has already "retired" twice.
